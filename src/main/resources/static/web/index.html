Body Building

Bodybuilding is a discipline that aims to burn fat and build muscle by lifting weights and performing anaerobic exercises. The idea of this practice is that the person is left with a marked, strong and defined body. Also maintain a symmetry between the torso and the legs.

Yoga

Yoga is a practice that connects the body, breath and mind. This practice uses physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to improve overall health. It helps among other things to lower your blood pressure and heart rate, help you relax, improve your self-confidence, reduce stress, improve your coordination, improve your concentration, help you sleep better, and more.

Spinning

Spinning is a collective physical exercise, which is performed on a stationary bicycle to the rhythm of music, in which high-intensity cardiovascular work is carried out with very high intervention of the large muscle groups of the lower body.

Kick-boxing

Kick boxing is a contact sport that alternates boxing blows and martial arts kicks. Generally, this name is used to refer to those exercises that combine kicks and punches. It is a great sport that combines strength, resistance and aerobic exercise, which makes it very complete and beneficial.

Crossfit

CrossFit is a type of functional exercise training, constantly varied, executed at high intensity. It is a program of strength and total physical conditioning, which is based on the increase of the ten most recognized physical capacities by specialists in sports training with weights. During the exercise, the aim is to develop muscle strength and tone and increase the functionality of the muscles to repeat the movements.
